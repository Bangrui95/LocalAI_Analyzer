Browser Extension

LocalAI Analyzer is a browser extension designed to run entirely on your local device, providing comprehensive analysis of your browsing history without relying on any external services.

By processing browsing activity directly on the device, the extension constructs a transparent and personalized interest profile while supplying the data foundation for an adaptive recommendation page.

Users can review, edit, and refine every personalized tag generated by the system, ensuring full control over their individual preference signals. No user data is ever transmitted to remote servers; all computation and storage occur exclusively on the user’s own device.

Local Model

Data is handled exclusively by on-device models, preserving the security and privacy of personal information

The open-source model sentence-transformers — all-mpnet-base-v2 is used for local analysis. Despite its compact size, it offers high accuracy and supports long-text processing. When deployed as a local backend, it runs efficiently while ensuring that all data processing stays strictly on the device, with no privacy leakage.

IAB Taxonomy

Employs a structured labeling system consistent with established industry taxonomies

Built to match industry-standard taxonomies, the system outputs multi-tier labels (Tier 1–3) and gives users full control to edit label definitions and weighting, enabling a personalized and adaptable classification structure.

Customizable analytical workflow

Apply a custom analysis process to the browsing history exported from your browser

The system exports a standardized JSON file of your browsing history and cleans and restructures its internal data. It then filters and preprocesses the content according to your personalized settings before passing it into the model for analysis. Users can customize blacklist rules, label granularity, and weighting strategies to generate interest labels that better align with their own preferences.
